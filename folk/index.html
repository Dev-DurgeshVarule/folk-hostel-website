About Us

Friends of Lord Krishna (FOLK) is a student-driven spiritual community inspired by the timeless teachings of Lord Krishna and guided by the vision of His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada.

We believe spirituality is not about isolation, but about friendship, learning, and shared experiences. Through trips, discussions, and devotional activities, we create a space where young minds grow with values, purpose, and devotion.

“Dedicated to His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada”
